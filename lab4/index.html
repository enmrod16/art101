Lab 4 - Pseudocoding and Problem Solving

Challenge

The challenge of the lab was to pseudocode an everyday task, and then pseudcode a simple computer game. The goal was to start thinking of pseudocoding before actually getting into the real coding of an item.

Problems

For this lab, we only had problems when we were attempting to pseudocode a game. We had a lot of ideas, but settled on pseudocoding the classic snake game. We had to figure out how to explain the loss condition and the scoring of points but through some back and forth we got to a satisfactory explanation.
